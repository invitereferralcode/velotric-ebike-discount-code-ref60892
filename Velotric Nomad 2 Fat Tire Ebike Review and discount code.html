Velotric Nomad 2 Fat Tire Ebike Review

The Velotric Nomad 2 is built for adventure, offering a perfect blend of power, comfort, and versatility. Whether you're tackling off-road trails or commuting through the city, this fat tire e-bike ensures a smooth ride.

Powerful Performance

Equipped with a 750W motor (peaking at 1300W) and 90Nm of torque, the Nomad 2 delivers excellent acceleration and hill-climbing ability. Its 48V, 14.7Ah (705.6Wh) Samsung/LG battery provides up to 65 miles of range with pedal assist.

Exceptional Ride Quality

The bike features 26×4.0" Kenda puncture-resistant fat tires for stability on all terrains. The RST hydraulic suspension fork with 100mm travel and a suspension seatpost ensure a smooth and comfortable ride.

Enhanced Safety Features

With Tektro hydraulic disc brakes (203mm front, 180mm rear), the Nomad 2 provides strong stopping power. Integrated lighting includes a 500-lumen headlight with auto-sensing capabilities and a rear light with brake and turn signal indicators.

Comfort and Customization

Designed for riders 5'2" to 6'5", the Nomad 2 comes in Regular and Large sizes. An adjustable stem allows fine-tuning for optimal riding posture.

Advanced Technology

A 3.5" full-color display provides ride data and Bluetooth connectivity. The bike is also compatible with Apple’s Find My™ network for added security.

Durability and Load Capacity

With a triple-butted aluminum frame and 505-lb payload capacity, the Nomad 2 is built for cargo and towing. The MIK-HD cargo rack holds up to 66 lbs.

Pricing and Availability

The Velotric Nomad 2 is available for $1,999 in multiple colors. Choose between high-step and step-thru frame options.
